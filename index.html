Salman Siddique Khan

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at the Department of ECE, Rice University. I am being advised by Dr. Ashok Veeraraghavan and Dr. Ashu Sabharwal. I work in the area of machine learning, computer vision and computational imaging. I finished my Ph.D. from the Computational Imaging Lab, IITM, where I worked under Dr. Kaushik Mitra on computational photography and computer vision. As a Ph.D. candidate, I was awarded the Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship for the year 2020-2021 to work on mask-based lensless cameras. Prior to joining the Ph.D. program at IIT Madras, I completed my undergraduate studies in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Rourkela, India.

Research

I'm primarily interested in developing machine learning, computer vision and computational imaging tools. Most of my recent works has been focused on using deep learning to develop novel imaging and image processing pipelines for systems with form-factor, cost and privacy advantages that can have applications in robotics, healthcare and smart wearables.
